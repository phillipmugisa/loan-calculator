Loan calculator

A loan calculator is a financial planning tool that allows you to compare different loan repayment methods. The loan calculator in front of you will calculate for you the amount of your expected monthly repayments based on the loan amount you set, or alternatively, will calculate the amount of the loan you can receive according to the monthly amount you can allocate for loan repayment.

The data can be entered in the windows at the bottom of the columns or using the scroll bars.

The Spitzer repayment method divides the total loan amount*, consisting of principal and interest, into regular monthly payments for the entire loan period.

In loans linked to an index or a foreign exchange rate, the loan calculator will update the loan repayment payments according to the expected differences.

The equal principal repayment method divides the principal component of the loan* into fixed monthly payments for the entire term of the loan.

This repayment method is characterized by decreasing payments, because the smaller the principal, so is the current interest derived from it.

In loans linked to the index or the foreign exchange rate, the differences will be added to the loan repayment amount.
